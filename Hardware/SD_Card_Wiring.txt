1. Connect the 5V pin to the 5V pin on the Arduino
2. Connect the GND pin to the GND pin on the Arduino
3. Connect CLK to pin 13 or 52.
4. Connect DO to pin 12 or 50.
5. Connect DI to pin 11 or 51.
6. Connect CS to pin 10 or 53.
